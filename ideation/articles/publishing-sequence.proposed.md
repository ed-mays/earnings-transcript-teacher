# Proposed Publishing Sequence

## Overview

Four parallel tracks, each targeting a distinct audience, all drawn from the same project.

| Track | Audience | Monetization Path |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| **Diary** | Cross-audience | Newsletter anchor, personal brand, future book |
| **Building With AI** | Developers | CodeMentor credibility, developer newsletter |
| **LLMs in Production** | Applied AI engineers | Consulting, workshops, technical authority |
| **Non-Coder's Playbook** | PMs, founders | Substack growth, course/cohort product |

**Cadence:** One article every 10–14 days (~4–5 months for the full sequence).

---

*Goal: Set up the narrative, build your voice, get reps in.*

| # | Track | Title | Rationale |

## Cross-Linking Structure

- Every **Track 2 (LLMs in Production)** piece links to the relevant **Track 1 (Building With AI)** piece for coding context
- Every **Track 3 (Non-Coder's Playbook)** piece links to the relevant **Track 1** piece for readers who want the technical detail
- Every **Diary** piece links forward to upcoming articles and back to what prompted the reflection
- Author bio on every article links to Article 1 as the "start here" entry point

## Article 1 Notes

**Why:** Opens the whole series and anchors all future cross-links. Must establish motivation, project scope, and the parallel tracks without naming them formally.

**Three motivations to weave together:**
1. Improve understanding of finance at enterprise scale
2. Learn AI-driven app development beyond basic chat interfaces
3. Explore AI use cases for non-technical roles

**Reader feeling at end:** Curious and inspired.

**Voice:** Warmest of the four tracks — first person, candid, honest about what you didn't know going in. Neutral and professional overall, with slight shifts per track.

**Opening hook decision (unresolved):**
- Option A — Lead with the gap: positions against the "I built a chatbot" pattern; resonates with developers
- Option B — Lead with the domain: more surprising, less likely to read as another AI tools article; broader reach

**Recommended:** Option B for broader top-of-funnel appeal.
